Who we are

Our company was founded in 2013 in Munich. Our mission is to provide secure email that is easy to use and runs on all devices. Our products are based on open standards and our source code is published for inspection. We also believe in contributing to existing open source projects that we build upon in our software.

Oliver Gajek

Oliver Gajek previously co-founded Brainloop AG, the leading cloud provider for document security. He loves telling people how to run their startups. Every now and then he will even follow his own advice. In his spare time he reads many books and buys even more.

Tankred Hase

Tankred Hase has been working on privacy by design software since 2011 and is a contributor to many Open Source projects. He believes that JavaScript, a Chromebook, and GitHub is all the IT you will ever need. In his spare time Tankred enjoys traveling by backpack to far away places.

Felix Hammerl

Felix Hammerl earned his chops on iOS apps for deployment in fine Bavarian automobiles. He believes that beautiful code and a rigorous development process go hand in hand and that you can’t have one without the other. In his spare time Felix bangs the drums and rides all sorts of bikes.

Andris Reinman

Andris Reinman is a Javascript developer from Estonia and the master of email protocols and servers. He is the author of email.js, the leading library for writing email apps on client and server in Javascript. His young family keeps him sane when dealing with the overwhelming complexities of the IMAP protocol.
