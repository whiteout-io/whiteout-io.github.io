Support

How to Contact Support:

Drop us an email, encrypted or not, at mail.support@whiteout.io. We love hearing from you and will try to get back to you as soon as possible.

How to Report a Bug:

Please use the “Report a Bug” option in the Whiteout Mail client. That way we can get important data about your systems platform and current state. Note that this does not transmit any message data or address book entries to us.
